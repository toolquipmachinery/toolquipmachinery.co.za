Our History & Vision

Toolquip & Machinery started in 2023 with Level 1 BEE status in Johannesburg as an SME. We are striving to become South Africa's number one supplier to the Manufacturing, Engineering, Mining & Industrial sectors, with a consistent commitment to quality and customer service.

Leadership Experience

Indren Chetty, a member of Toolquip & Machinery (Pty) Ltd, brings over 30 years of industry experience. Formerly an employee of the original Toolquip & Allied Company, he has held various roles including Sales & Branch Manager for several companies. This extensive background enables us to deeply understand customer needs, demands, and deadlines.
